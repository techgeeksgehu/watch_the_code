# About Us

### The University

<div class="bg-white/50 backdrop-blur-xl rounded-4xl p-5 md:p-10">

<Image src={GEHU} alt="Graphic Era Hill University" class="w-full h-auto object-contain rounded-t-2xl" pictureAttributes={{style: "margin-top: 0;"}}/>

Graphic Era Hill University (GEHU), established in 2011 in Uttarakhand, India,
aims to provide world-class education focusing on advanced technologies,
professional development, critical thinking, and significant research. The
university has earned a reputation for academic excellence, producing high
achieving students and guided by a qualified faculty.

</div>

### The Campus

<div class="bg-white/50 backdrop-blur-xl rounded-4xl p-5 md:p-10">

<Image src={GEHUHaldwani} alt="GEHU Haldwani Campus" class="w-full h-auto object-contain rounded-t-2xl" pictureAttributes={{style: "margin-top: 0;"}}/>

The GEHU Haldwani Campus combines
natural beauty with urban vitality, featuring
an amphitheater, cafeterias, sports fields, a
library, labs, computer centers, and lecture
theaters. It offers a vibrant academic
atmosphere and diverse courses, making it
an ideal place for a transformative
educational experience.

</div>

### The Club

<div class="bg-white/50 backdrop-blur-xl rounded-4xl p-5 md:p-10">

<Image src={TG} alt="Tech Geeks Club" class="w-full h-auto object-contain rounded-t-2xl" pictureAttributes={{style: "margin-top: 0;"}}/>

Founded in 2020, the Tech Geeks Club is
GEHU Haldwani's official technology club,
formed by enthusiastic tech students. It
organizes events such as Hackathons,
Competitive Programming Contests, Tech
Quizzes, and Workshops, providing a platform
for learning, innovation, and talent showcase
in computer science.

</div>
